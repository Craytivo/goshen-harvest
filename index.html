Preventative Maintenance Services Provided

Our comprehensive service covers all your most-used equipment. We go beyond quick fixes—our checklist keeps your machines performing at their best to give your members a seamless gym experience.

Bikes

  • • Full test ride to assess real-user performance.
  • • Inspect pedals, crank arms, display, keypad, and chain tension.
  • • Lubricate drivetrain components as needed.
  • • Check and tighten all exterior bolts for safety.

Treadmills

  • • Test run and inspect power functionality and safety systems.
  • • Evaluate belt and deck wear, wax level, and alignment.
  • • Remove motor cover and vacuum dust/debris buildup.
  • • Calibrate speed and incline levels for accuracy.

Ellipticals

  • • Test functionality with a full user session.
  • • Inspect pedals, arms, displays, and motion smoothness.
  • • Lubricate internal linkages and rails as needed.
  • • Perform full hardware bolt and casing safety check.

Steppers

  • • Check for rust, wear, and bubbling under high stress areas.
  • • Inspect and adjust chain or belt tension mechanisms.
  • • Degrease and lubricate moving components.
  • • Verify pedal integrity and machine stability.

Rowers

  • • Clean monorail thoroughly for consistent glide.
  • • Inspect pedals, arms, handle tension, and chain condition.
  • • Apply lubricant to pivot points if necessary.
  • • Tighten all mounting hardware and connection bolts.

Weight Machines

  • • Test movement and range of motion under load.
  • • Inspect cables, guide rods, and frame stability.
  • • Lubricate weight stack and adjustment pins.
  • • Ensure all safety labels and guards are intact.
